Searching

gGo offers easy searching for moves in the current game or variation branch:

  • When in normal mode, holding down the shift key and left clicking moves to the first position of the main variation branch where the stone at this spot was played.

  • When in normal mode, holding down the shift key and right clicking moves to the next position within the current branch where the stone at this spot was played.
